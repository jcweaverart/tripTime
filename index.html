    The journey of a life time is waiting in for you in just 3 simple steps.

    CREAT YOUR PROFILE, SET YOUR BUDGET, TAKE YOUR TRIP

    Start Here

    Join us by signing up today. You will create your profile and choose some custom trip preferences. Each trip you set will have custom questions that help us get an idea of exactly the trip you want to plan for.

    Budgeting begins here

    Once you begin your budgeting journey with us, we help you by setting a minumum amount for you to invest each month. You set what your budget goal is and we notify you every time you reach a certain level on your budget! Every time you invest you get a certain amount of rewards coins.

    Take Your Trip

    Once you have met your budget goal it's time to exchange your coins for your perfect trip! Wether you want to match up with one of our travel agency partners or layout each component of your trip, you have full control.
